Venue

Hotel Contempo is the perfect spot for a gathering of modern artists. For those who opt to stay at another location, there is certainly no shortage of hotels in Downtown Seattle. Ranging from shabby chic to the ultimate in sophistication - there is a something to suit everyone's taste.

These hotels include:

  • GoldenDragon Inn
  • Philips of Belltown
  • The Otter Renaissance Hotel
  • The Rage Hotel
  • Gwendoline's Fancy
